Our PlushPuppies are raised in a warm and loving home environment.

From birth, our puppies are surrounded by care and affection, ensuring they feel secure and develop into happy individuals.

  • We provide a clean, comfortable, and stimulating environment for our puppies to grow and explore.
  • Our days are filled with cuddles, playtime, and gentle interaction to nurture their development.
  • We believe a loving start is essential for a puppy's future happiness and well-being.

We strive to create a foundation of trust and comfort for each of our PlushPuppies before they embark on their new adventures with their forever families.

Our puppies are raised as part of our family, receiving constant attention and care.

Being family-raised means our PlushPuppies are well-accustomed to the everyday sounds and activities of a home, making their transition to your family much smoother.

They interact regularly with adults and children, learning valuable social skills and developing gentle temperaments.

  • They experience the warmth and companionship of human interaction from their earliest days.
  • Our family is dedicated to providing a nurturing and enriching environment for each puppy.
  • We believe this close interaction helps our PlushPuppies become well-adjusted and loving companions.
  • They learn to feel secure and loved within a family setting.

The health and well-being of our PlushPuppies are of paramount importance to us.

We ensure all our puppies receive regular veterinary care, including health checks, initial vaccinations, and deworming.

  • Each puppy is examined by a licensed veterinarian before going to their new home.
  • We provide you with a record of their vaccinations and health history.
  • We are committed to responsible breeding and ensuring healthy, happy puppies.

We are always available to answer any questions you may have about the health and ongoing care of your new PlushPuppy.

We believe early socialization is crucial for our PlushPuppies to develop into well-mannered and friendly dogs.

From a young age, our puppies are introduced to a variety of sights, sounds, people, and gentle handling.

This early exposure helps them build confidence and adapt more easily to new environments and experiences in their future lives.

  • They interact daily with our family, including children of different ages.
  • We introduce them to different textures, toys, and common household sounds.
  • Our goal is to ensure our PlushPuppies are well-socialized and ready to become loving members of your family.
