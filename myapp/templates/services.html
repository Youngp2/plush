Our Services

How We Care for Your Future Companion

Loving Home Raising

Our puppies are raised in our home with constant love and attention, ensuring they are well-adjusted to family life from day one.

Comprehensive Health Care

We prioritize the health of our puppies, providing thorough vet checks, initial vaccinations, and deworming before they go to their new homes. Health records are provided.

Early Socialization Program

Our puppies are introduced to various sights, sounds, people, and gentle handling from a young age to develop friendly and confident personalities.

Guidance on Adoption

We provide guidance and support throughout the adoption process to ensure our puppies find the perfect loving forever homes.

Post-Adoption Support

We offer ongoing support and are available to answer any questions you may have after you welcome one of our PlushPuppies into your family.

Information and Resources

We provide helpful resources and information on puppy care, training tips, and breed-specific details to help you and your new companion thrive.
